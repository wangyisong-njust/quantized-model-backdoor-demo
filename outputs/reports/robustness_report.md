## Key Findings

- **FP32 ASR = 8.3%**, **INT8 ASR = 9.6%** (Δ = +1.3%): quantization slightly increases attack vulnerability
- **Latency**: FP32 GPU=3.5ms, INT8 CPU(ORT)=26.6ms (different backends, not directly comparable)
- **DPatch vanishing rate = 4.7%** on COCO val2017 (fraction of clean detections suppressed by the patch)
